Monitoring Crops Growth

From the early stage of development through budding and up to harvesting, it is very important to monitor plant health in order to timely detect any problem.


Accurate Weather Prediction

Climate conditions have a predominant influence on the development of plants and, consequently, on yields.


Crop Protection Methods

In general, there are various crop protection methods. Among the main ones are weed/pest management and plant disease management.


Soil Testing & Its Quality

To attain increased crop yields, farmers need to closely monitor soil conditions on their land plots. One of the best ways to maintain the soil in good health is to practice crop rotation, among others.
